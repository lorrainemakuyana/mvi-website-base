#MVIExperience

A chance to set yourself on course, bringing out the best you.

The MVI Experience as we call it, is one you must experience because it sets you apart from the rest.

You can have your very own experience by being part of one of our programs listed below.

The MVI Fellows Network

This Program is created and designed for young women and girls aged 15-21 years. It gives a first-hand experience of MVI as a whole and delivered by the MVI Team and industry experts in chosen fields to inspire and motivate young women and girls into becoming dreamchasers, changemakers, and fearless women who take up space. The program is offered mostly virtually via Zoom and WhatsApp, and will soon be launched on a downloadable application or webpage. Stipends to cater for data costs will be provided for participants and although rigorous and intensive, this program is so far the best way to access MVI resources and interact with us first hand.

How do I join?

Great question! Below is the step by step guide into how selections are made how you can be a part of our next cohort!

  1. Call for applications. Applications calls are usually advertised on our Instagram Page and you can apply via the link that will be shared. Also, you can reach out to us via Email to find out if applications are open and to request a link to apply.

  2. Apply. Fill in the application form before the due date to apply for a spot on the program. Whilst applying, take care to highlight your experiences, why you want to join the program, your career goals, how MVI will help you achieve them, and your goals as a future leader. Also, try to join a live info session to hear from our team first hand what we are looking out for in your application.

  3. Application Review. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. We will reach out to all applications via email latest two weeks after the application deadline. If shortlisted, you will be invited for a virtual interview with one or two of our team members.

  4. Final Review. After interviews are done and reviewed, MVI will select and roll out the final participant list for the particular cohort. All interviewees should expect to hear from MVI within a week after their interview.

  5. Program starts The program is launched.

If you do not hear from My Vision Initiative three weeks after you submit your applications or two weeks after your interview, write to us by email or contact us on WhatsApp at +263781939238 or call +263776936464.
